Privacy Policy for AgriBackup

Effective Date: October 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how AgriBackup ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and other details you provide during registration or profile updates.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates and location information for features like zone management, pickup scheduling, and mapping interactions.
  • Produce and Farm Data: Details about your farm, produce listings, yield records, harvest data, and uploaded images.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including device information, IP address, and analytics data collected via Firebase.
  • Feedback and Communications: Messages, feedback submissions, and support requests you send to us.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Functionality: To provide and maintain the app's core features, such as farmer registration, produce management, buyer matching, and order tracking.
  • Security: To authenticate users, prevent fraud, and ensure secure access to your account.
  • Analytics: To analyze app usage, improve performance, and develop new features.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Communication: To send notifications, updates, and respond to your inquiries.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating the app (e.g., Firebase for analytics and hosting), under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or legal request.
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition.
  • With your consent, for specific purposes you have agreed to.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and databases managed through our backend systems. We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits, to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: Withdraw consent for certain data processing, such as analytics or marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at agribackupltd@gmail.com. For account deletion, visit our delete account page.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information promptly.
