How to delete your AgriBackup account

This page explains how AgriBackup users can request account deletion and the types of data that will be removed or retained.

Steps to request account deletion

  1. Log in to your AgriBackup account at agribackup.com.
  2. Go to Settings > Account and choose Delete Account (if available).
  3. If you cannot access your account, email us at agribackupltd@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. Include your full name and user ID if known.
  4. We will verify your request and process the deletion within 7 business days.
Important: Once deletion is complete, you will not be able to recover your account or data unless you create a new account.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile information (name, contact details, profile photo).
  • Your farm(s) and farm profile data.
  • Uploaded produce records and images (subject to backup retention described below).
  • Produce yields and related harvest records created by you.
  • Any non-financial activity logs tied to your account.

What may be retained

For legal and regulatory reasons we may retain some records even after an account is deleted. Examples include:

  • Payment and transaction history: retained for up to 24 months to satisfy accounting and tax regulations.
  • Records required by law or dispute resolution (may be retained longer as required).
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data that cannot be used to identify you.

Questions or disputes

If you have questions about account deletion or you believe deletion was performed in error, contact support at agribackupltd@gmail.com. Provide relevant details so we can investigate.

This page was created to satisfy Google's requirement for a publicly accessible account deletion URL. Keep a copy of this URL: https://agribackup.com/delete-account.
